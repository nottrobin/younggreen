Contact the Committee

Our National Committee look after the day-to-day running of the organisation, supporting local groups and members among other things. It is elected each year in Autumn at our Annual Convention and consists of two co-chairs, Treasurer and a number of general members who work together to ensure all tasks are looked after. It meets regularly to set strategy, the budget and review the progress of the organisation.

The Young Greens Committee consists of nine posts: two gender balanced Co-Chairs, Treasurer and 6 general members. For a short biography, please click on the member's name.

Get Involved!

All of our national committee members are volunteers, and are busy with work or study as well as with Young Greens. If you have a bit of time, and would like to contribute to a particular area of YG, please do contact Larissa (admin@younggreens.org.uk) or get in touch with the relevant committee member directly. Don’t forget that there are lots of opportunities to get involved with Local Groups as well.

It's election season! Join a Young Greens action weekend

10 April 2013

Election season is coming up and there are plenty of Young Greens standing. With less than a month to go, please get involved with a Young Green action weekend if you can and help us win more seats!

Bristol action weekend - 20th/21st April

We are targetting three wards - one of which we have won before and two in which we are competitive. Rob Telford and Anna McMullen are both young Greens and are working hard to become city councillors. You can canvass or leaflet, all are welcome and we can find you somewhere to stay overnight.

We will all go somewhere together on the Saturday night and relive 2012's autumn conference!

If you're interested or want any more information, please contact Rob Telford atrobertjessetelford@gmail.com

 

Norwich action weekend - 13th/14th April

Norwich is the second city for the Green Party and this May we're fighting six defences on the County Council and hoping to gain back a seat from a Tory defector. We've also got a by-election for a seat on the City Council which we hope to win. (Yes, it is always this busy around here). The elections are important in their own right, but we're also using them to build up towards the general election for which Norwich South is a national target constituency.

Saturday will be canvassing-based, with leafleting for people who don't want to canvass. Full training will be given for anyone who hasn't canvassed before, and we'll send you out with an experienced councillor or activist. In the evening we'll have a social event and a chance for you to meet councillors and activists in Norwich. On the Sunday we will put on some training on running election campaigns for anyone who wants it. We'll cover things like the principles of targeting, what to do at different points of the campaign and how to pick your issues for the electipn.

Contact Ash Haynes (ashlizhaynes@gmail.com) if you want to come along and help get some Young Greens elected in Norwich as well as learning lots about the way elections are run!

 

Oxford action weekend - 27/28th April

Our Co-Chair Sam Coates is working hard to became a Councillor in Oxford! Fellow Young Green and 30 under 30 member Sam Hollick was elected easily in part of the ward last May but the County Council is being fought for under new boundaries. Previous results suggest the seat is going to be a close fight between Greens and Labour but if enough of us help out we can get Sam elected on 2 May.

If elected Sam wants to be a voice for public services and against the privitisation that the Tory County are all too keen to follow Cameron on. It will also be a big boost for the Oxford Greens to win this new seat to continue expanding in this very green city!

The ward is majority student but very diverse so you will get the chance to speak to students or local residents. We'll be doorknocking, talking to people on the street and leafleting for those who prefer it. We'll provide training before we start.

All Young Greens are warmly welcomed to our specially organised action weekend on the 27/28th April in Oxford. For more details contact sam@younggreens.org.uk or join our Facebook event.

We also have action days planned most weekends between now and the election, if you can help out another time do get in touch, we always have work that needs doing!

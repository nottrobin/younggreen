We have a vacancy on the Young Greens committee and are looking for applicants!

21 March 2013

Joining the committee is a great way to get more involved with the running of the Young Greens and to meet people who are committed to helping young people get involved in politics and making the future of the Green Party a young, and forward-looking one.

The Young Greens committee sets the direction of the organsation, helps to organise events, decide on campaigns, produce publications and decide on new projects. We hold a fortnightly Skype meeting and occasional face-to-face meetings in London (travel costs will be covered, and accommodation offered). The vacancy is in one of our six non-portfolio roles.

General Committee Responsibilities

  • Attend as many committee meetings as possible or give apologies
  • Take part in email discussion/votes on things that arise between meetings, where appropriate
  • Help promote the Young Greens at any event you attend as a committee member, such as conferences or student events
  • Help Young Green members with their work in local groups, student societies or individually where it relates to your role
  • Inform the co-chairs and/or coordinator if you are having difficulties with any part of your post or need help

We are particularly looking for people who are interested in working on member support, but are mostly looking for anyone with enthusiasm and some time to offer. Other roles include Events, Campaigns, Press, Publications and International.

Member Support responsibilities include

  • Assist with Fresher's Fairs
  • Help new groups or societies in setting up
  • Develop recruitment strategies for local groups and nationally
  • Ensure Young Greens representation at NUS and other student events
  • Encourage sharing of ideas and good practice between groups
  • Work with Publications, Campaigns and Events coordinators to provide ideas and help to groups in these areas

If you are interested please send statement (no longer than 300 words), explaining why you would like to join the committee and which area you would be interested in working in to Larissa atadmin@younggreens.org.uk. Feel free to contact the same address with any questions about the role, no matter how small!

The deadline for applications is midday on Wednesday 3rd April. All applicants will be asked to briefly introduce themselves via Skype on 4th April between 9-10.30pm.

Young Greens Summer Gathering

1 May 2013

This year we are hosting our first ever Young Greens Summer Gathering. We're going to be gathering one hundred Young Greens from Scotland, Ireland, England and Wales to host a fully funded five day event on Direct Democracy and Citizenship.

This is a great chance take part in workshops and hear talks from expert speakers on Green issues, direct democracy, Europe and citizenship. There will also be socials, intercultural evenings and plenty of time to meet with, share experiences and build capacity between Young Green activists from Ireland, Scotland and England and Wales.

The event will be held between the 18th and 22nd of July 2013 in Gloucestershire and is financially supported by the Federation of Young European Greens. Food, accomodation and travel will all be covered.

If you think exploring and learning about these topics with other Young Green activists from the UK and Ireland sounds unbelievably amazing, you'll need to fill out a (short) application form and send it to admin@younggreens.org.uk. The deadline for this is the 17th May 2013.

The Summer Gathering preparation team in cooperation with respective Young Green Executive Committees will select the participants in late May 2013.

The form, conditions of application and lots more information can be found at http://younggreens.greenparty.org.uk/events/summercamp.
